About Me

Shamya Zindani is a student at Fordham University Lincon Center. She is studying New Media and Digital Design with a concentration in New Media and Commerce. Shamya is currently on the Executive Board of Fordham Lincon Cneter's school newspaper, The Observer, as the Social Media Manager and Assistant Photo Editor. She is interested in User Experience Design Research and finding methods of making future design inclusive and thoughtful. Reach out to szindani@fordham.edu to connect.
